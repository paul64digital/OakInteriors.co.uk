Factory Visits


The following are details about our visits we make every year to the furniture factories:

This is your unique opportunity to join a small group to visit one of the world's largest oak furniture manufacturers, in a relaxed and informal atmosphere, and to gain a facinating and valuable insight into furniture making. By seeing, first hand, exactly how furniture is made, you can be sure you made the right choice, first time.

The KM factory is set on an impressive 86 acre site, and includes a stunning 17,000sq ft showroom offering inspiration for your home.

Beautiful Bruges
The trip includes an afternoon and overnight stop in the romantic city of Bruges, with four star bed and breakfast accommodation in a centrally located hotel. One of Europe's best preserved medieval cities, a stroll through Bruges' maze of cobbled streets is a facinating experience.
Shops, bars and resturants are plentiful - chocolate lovers will be in heaven, beer drinkers in paradise!
An opitional 45 minute guided tour of the city centre is included in the trip.
