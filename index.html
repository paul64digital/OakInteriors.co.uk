Dining room furniture - modern.

MILANO

Made in Belgium, MILANO is available in natural or washed oak. Cabinets feature solid oak fronts, with tops and ends in real oak veneers with solid lippings. Two sizes of extending table are available, to seat 4-8 or 6-10, both featuring an ingenious self storing leaf. The MILANO chair is solid oak and comes with a choice of fabrics. Clear or frosted glass, or wooden doors can be fitted to the cabinets. Glass shelves are toughened.

SKOVBY

An extensive range of dining furniture from Denmark, featuring clean, modern design, and a fantastic selection of tables. Skovby specialise in versatile tables with innovative extension mechanisms which have won numerous design awards over the years.
All items are manufactured from a combination of solids and veneers, and most are available in a choice of woods including oak, beech, maple and cherry.

XENON

Made in Belgium, 'Xenon' is designed in the 'cubic' style which is currently very popular in Holland and Belgium. Tables and cabinets are made from a combination of solids and veneers, with chairs in solid oak. There are two ranges which can be used together or independantly - the freestanding range shown here, and a new modular range. The Xenon table can be ordered in two fixed top sizes, or as an extending version with two leaves.

CREAFORT

Made in Holland, this is a high quality range of predominantly solid contempory furniture. The range includes an ingenious solid extending table (made in various sizes) which is one of very few truly contemporary AND solid AND extending tables on the market. The range includes sideboards, bookcases, display cabinets, and occasional furniture, all in a choice of two finishes.



Dining room furniture - classic.

SAFIR

Safir is one of our most popular ranges, and is constructed from a combination of solids and veneers. Cabinets feature solid oak fronts, with horizontal and vertical panels in oak veneers with solid lippings. This extensive range is available in four colours and consists of a free-standing range for the dining room, and a modular range ideal for the lounge.
Fixed top and extending solid oak tables, together with a wide choice of chairs, complement the range.

PROVENCE

The Provence table is consistantly in our top 5 sellers list. Chunky and practical, it is made entirely from solid European Oak - which means that looked after, you'll never need another dining table. Six sizes, with the option of extension leaves, and a range of chairs offering informal or formal dining. The matching cabinets are also entirely solid. Oiled, waxed, washed, and stained finishes. Provence is rustic in style, fantastic value, and built to last.



YORK

Dinner for two?

Dinner party for 20?

This solid oak table seats any number from 2-20. Probably our most flexible table, it can be ordered with anything from 1-8 extension leaves - allowing it to extend to a remarkable 4.7m. Remove the extension leaves, drop the flaps, and it closes to just 66cm x 120cm. Hand made in Holland using 150 year old French character oak, it is available in various finishes, and in three widths. various chairs are available to match and sideboards in three sizes.

ANTIX

100% solid oak, distressed furniture in a wide choice of finishes, Antix is made in the south of France. The huge range includes over 40 table designs in a wealth of sizes, with chairs, benches, sideboards, cabinets and occasional furniture to match.
The distressed finish makes this furniture virtually child proof - scratches and dents can easily be dealt with using wire wool and wax, more severe damage can be sanded back and easily repolished. Very popular with growing families!


ANTIX MISTRAL

Slightly more contemporary in style, Antix Mistral is available in the same finishes - so you can mix and match across both ranges. 100% solid oak.


VENEZIA

One of our best selling ranges, venezia is made in Belgium, and is available in eleven shades of oak from natural to dark. Cabinets feature solid fronts, end panels, plinths, frames and moldings. Circular, extending, and rectangular fixed tables, with a choice of four chair designs complete the range.

About us:

Founded in 1918 by Harriet Smith, the first shop sold nursery goods and furniture. Harriet passed the business down to her sons Cecil and Albert, who concentrated on furniture, both new and used. Cecil's son Dennis joined in 1957 - and is still working a 6 day week, 44 years on! He expanded the business to a second store, and widened the product range to include carpets, and upholstery. By the early 90's, however, it became necessary to specialize to survive in the ever more competitive furniture trade. Thus the birth of Oak Interiors.
